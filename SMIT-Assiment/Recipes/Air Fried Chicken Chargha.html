Air Fried Chicken Chargha Recipe

We challenged our selves and make the chargha we all love healthy by removing the need to deep fry. Thanks to Philips air fryer we were able to get perfect results!

Ingredients;

  • Whole chicken approx 1kg.
  • Water 18% litre (enough to dip chicken).
  • Sirka (Vinegar) 2 tbs.
  • Namak (Salt) 2 tsp.
  • Paprika powder 1 tsp.
  • Ajwain (Carom seeds) crushed ½ tsp.
  • Dhania powder (Coriander powder) % tos.

  • Haldi powder (Turmeric powder) ½ tsp.
  • Chaat masala 18% tsp.
  • Lal mirch (Red chill) crushed & The Zarda ka rang (Orange food color) ¼ tsp.
  • Corn Flour 1 tbsp -Adrak lehsan paste (Ginger Garlic paste) 16% The Mustard paste 1 tbs.
  • Loman juice 2-3 tbs -Cooking oil 2 tsp.
  • Oil spray.
  • Lal mirch (Red chilli) crushed.
  • Lemon juice -Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped.

Directions:

  • Make deep cuts on whole chicken & set asida
  • In a bowladd water, vinegar,salt & mix welt
  • Add whole chicken cover &marinate for 30 minutes then strain, pat dry & set aside
  • In a bowladd paprika powder,carom seeds,coriander powder,salt,cumin powder turmeric powder, chaat masala,red chilli crushed.cornflour. orange food color ginger garlic paste.mustard pastejemon juice & mix well
  • Add cooking oil & mix well
  • Add whole chicken & apply marinade evenly & massage chicken wallitio chicken legs with kitchen thread cover & marinate for 2-4 hours & set aside
  • in a wokadd water & bring it to boil.
  • Place steamer & add marinated chicken cover & steam cook on low flame for 30-35 minutes
  • Take out chicken from steamer.spray oilplace it in air fryer basket & air fry at 180C for 15-18 minutes.
