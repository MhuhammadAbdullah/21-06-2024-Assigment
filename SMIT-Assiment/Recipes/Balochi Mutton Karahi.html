Balochin Mutton Karahi Recipe

Mouth watering Balochi Mutton Karahi Recipe!

Ingredients:

  1. Prepare Balochi Spice Mix:
    • Zeera (Cumin seeds) roasted & crushed 1 & ½ tbs
    • Sabut dhania (Coriander seeds) roasted & crushed 2 tbs
    • Lal mirch (Red chill) crushed 1 tbs
    • Chaat masala 1 tsp
    • Karahi masala 1 tbs
    • Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1 tsp
    • Kasuri methi (Dried fenugreek leaves) 1 tbs
    • Garam masala powder ½ tsp
    • Tamatar (Tamatoes) chopped 2 medium
    • Mutton mix boti 750g
    • Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste
    • Water 1 & ½ Cup
    • Cooking oil 5-6 tbs
    • Adrak (Ginger) chopped 2 & ½ tbs
    • Lehsan (Garlic) chopped 3 tbs
    • Hari mirch (Green chillies) 10-12
    • Lemon juice 2-3 tbs
  2. Prepare Dahi Kachomar Salad:
    • Kheera (Cucumber) chopped i medium -Tamatar (Tomatoes) chopped 2 medium
    • Pyaz (Onion) chopped 1 medium -Hari mirch (Green chillies) chopped 2-3
    • Dahi (Yogurt) whisked 1 CupHara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped
    • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped
    • Lemon juice

Directions:

  1. Prepare Spice Mix:
    • In a bowladd cumin seeds.coriander seeds, red chilli crushed chaat masala karahi masala black pepper powder dried fenugreek leaves garam masala powder & mix woll Balochi spice mix is ready!
    • In a pressure cooker add mutton mix botitomatoes, salt, water, mix well & bring it to boilcover and pressure cook on medium flame until 90% done (12-15 minutes) then cook on high flame until water is reduced & set aside.
    • In a wok add cooking oil and heat it.
    • Add ginger garlic and sauté until light goiden.
    • Add cooked mutton and mir woll
    • Slit green chillies from center add in wok,mix well & cook on medium flame for 8-10 minutes.
    • Add balochi spice mix 3-4 tbs.Jemon.cover & simmer on low flame for 1-2 minutes.
    • Garnish with fresh coriander add lernon juice serve with dahi kachomar salad & naan!
  2. Prepare Dahi Kochomar Salad:
    • In a bowl, add cucumber.tomatoes.onion.green chillies and mix well.
    • Add yogurt balochi spice mix 2 tsp & mix well -Garnish with fresh coriander & serve!
