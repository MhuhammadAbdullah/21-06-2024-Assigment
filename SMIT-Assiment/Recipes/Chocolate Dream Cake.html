Chocolate Dream Cake Recipe

Chocolate lovers Dream, this dream chocolate cake is one of our best cake recipes!

Ingredients:

  1. Prepare Chocolate Cake (Layer 1):
    • Anda (Egg) 1
    • Olper's Milk & Cup
    • Cooking oil Cup
    • Vanilla essence 1 tsp
    • Bareek cheeni (Caster sugar) & Cup
    • Maida (All-purpose flour) 1& & Cup
    • Cocoa powder & Cup
    • Himalayan pink salt Â½ tsp
    • Baking powder 1 tsp
    • Baking soda tsp
    • Hot water & Cup
  2. Prepare Chocolate Mousse (Layer 2).
    • Ice cubes as required
    • Olper's Cream chilled 250ml
    • Semi sweetened dark chocolate grated 150g
    • Icing sugar 4 tbs

Directions:

  1. Prepare Chocolate Cake (Layer 1):
    • In a bowladd egg milk.cooking oil vanilla essence caster sugar & beat well
    • On a bowl place a sieve add all-purpose flour.cocoa powder pink salt baking powder, baking soda & sift together then beat until well combined.
    • Add hot water & beat well
    • On greased 8-inch baking pan lined with butter paper.pour cake batter & tap few times.
    • Bake in preheated oven at 180C for 30 minutes (on lower grill).
  2. Let it cool at room temperatura. Prepare Chocolate Mousse (Layer 2)
    • In a large bowladd ice cubes, place another bowl in it add cream & beat for 3-4 minutes.
    • Add icing sugar, vanilla essence & beat until stiff peaks form.
    • In a another small bowladd dark chocolate, 3-4 tbs of cream & microwave for a minute then mix wall until smooth
    • Now add melted chocolate in cream mixture & beat until well combined.
  3. Transfer to a piping bag & refrigerate until use. Prepare Chocolate Top Shell (Layer 4)
    • In a bowladd dark chocolate.coconut oil & microwave for a minute then mix well until smooth
    • Remove cake from baking pan & trim cake as per cake tin size with the help of a round cutter (6.5" cake tin).
    • Place the coke in the bottom of tin box add sugar syrup & let it sook for 10 minutes.
    • Pipe out prepared chocolate mousse on the cake & spread evenly
    • Pipe out a thin layer of chocolate ganoche (layer 3) & spread evenly.
    • Pour melted chocolate, spread evenly & refrigerate until set.
    • Sprinkle cocoa powder & gift it to your loved once
