Namkeen Goshth Karahi Recipe

Mutton Namkeen Gosht karahi and must try this this bakra Eid!

Ingredients:

  • Cooking oil 1/3 Cup
  • Mutton mix boti 1 kg (with 10% fat)
  • Adrak (Ginger) crushed 1 tbsp
  • Lahsun (Garlic) crushed 1 tbs
  • Himalayan pink salt 1 tsp or to taste -Water 2-3 Cups
  • Sabut dhania (Coriander seeds) crushed 1 tbs
  • Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 18 ½ tsp
  • Hari mirch (Green chilli) crushed 1 tbs
  • Dahi (Yogurt) whisked 4 tbsp
  • Lemon juice ½ tbs
  • Adrak (Ginger) julienne 1-inch piece
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped 1-2 tbs
  • Hari mirch (Green chilli) sliced 1-2
  • Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped
  • Adrak (Ginger) julienne
  • Hari mirch (Green chilli) sliced

    • Directions:

    • In a cast iron pan,add cooking oil & heat it.
    • Add mutton,mix well & cook on high flame for 4-5 minutes.
    • Add ginger,garlic.pink salt, mix well & cook for 3-4 minutes.
    • Add water,mix well & bring it to boil,cover & cook on low flame until meat is tender (35-40 minutes).
    • Add coriander seeds, black pepper powder,green chilli, yogurt, mix well & cook on medium flame until oil separates (2-3 minutes).
    • Add lemon juice,ginger,fresh coriander,green chilli & mix well.
    • Garnish with fresh coriander,ginger,green chill & serve with naan!
