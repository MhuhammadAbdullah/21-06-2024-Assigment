Teriyaki Sesame Beef Skewers Recipe

Teriyaki Sesame Beef Skewers a perfect recipe to sizzle your taste buds!

Ingredients:

  • Beef steak undercut % kg
  • Lehsan (Garlic) crushed 1 tsp
  • Adrak (Ginger) crushed 1 tsp
  • Soy sauce 2 tbs
  • Sirka (Vinegar) 1 tbs
  • Hoisin sauce 1 tbs
  • Til ka tel (Sesame oil) 2 tsp
  • Brown sugar powdered 2 tbs
  • Namak (Salt) ½ tsp or to taste
  • Safed til (Sesame seeds) roasted 1 tbs
  • Kali mirch powder (Black pepper powder) 1½ tsp
  • Cooking oil 2 tbs
  • Safed til (Sesame seeds) toasted
  • Fresh parsley chopped

Directions:

  • Cut undercut beef into thin strips and; set aside.
  • In a bowl,add garlic,ginger,soy sauce,vinegar, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, salt,sesame seeds, black pepper powder and mix well.
  • Add beef strips and; mix well,cover and marinate for 2-3 hours.
  • Thread marinated beef onto the wooden skewers (makes 10-12).
  • Grease grill pan with cooking oil and: grill on medium flame until done (4-5 minutes each side).
  • Sprinkle sesame seeds, fresh parsley and; serve!
